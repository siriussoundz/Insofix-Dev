About Insofix

Insofix Ltd is an owner-led insulation-removal and remediation business helping homeowners understand difficult insulation enquiries.

What Insofix focuses on

The business focuses on spray-foam removal and related insulation-removal enquiries where access, surveys, moisture concerns or property transactions have made the next step uncertain. Additional services may include loft-insulation removal, cavity-wall extraction and replacement-insulation options.

Assessment before promises

Scope and expectations are discussed before work because removal depends on material, access, construction, visible condition and preparation. That approach avoids treating every loft or wall as the same.

Honest limitations

Insofix can discuss removal scope and provide work records, but does not control decisions made by lenders, valuers, surveyors, insurers or building professionals. If independent technical input is more appropriate, that should be made clear.

Removal, inspection and replacement

Removal may improve access to areas that were previously covered. Replacement insulation is considered separately, after ventilation and construction have been reviewed and any inspection needs are understood.

Communication and coverage

Insofix considers enquiries across England, with priority availability across Southern England. The aim is straightforward communication, written scope and useful completion information where work is carried out.
