Assessment and Remediation Process

The process separates an initial remote review from any chargeable on-site assessment or agreed removal work.

No automatic removal recommendation

Insofix helps homeowners establish what is actually required before they commit to expensive or potentially destructive work.

  1. Free Remote Photo Review

    Initial photographs and information help identify the most sensible next step.

  2. Chargeable On-Site Assessment

    Used where the property, foam type, roof condition or customer objective needs closer investigation.

  3. Options Explained

    Possible next steps and limitations are discussed clearly.

  4. Agreed Work

    Only the work accepted by the customer is undertaken.

  5. Post-Work Evidence

    Relevant photographs and completion information are provided for work actually completed.

Possible outcomes from an evidence-led review

The suitable outcome depends on the individual property, foam, access, visible condition and objective.

No immediate work recommended.
Obtain or clarify missing installation paperwork.
Further on-site assessment.
Monitoring or investigation.
Targeted remediation.
Controlled spray foam removal.
Roofing, timber or structural specialist input.
Replacement insulation after agreed removal.

Completion Evidence Pack

For agreed work, Insofix may provide a Completion Evidence Pack as relevant to the agreed scope.

  • Agreed work scope and exclusions.
  • Before, during and after photographs.
  • Areas accessed and areas that could not be accessed.
  • Foam type recorded where reasonably identifiable.
  • Work completed and visible observations made during the work.
  • Waste-handling or disposal information where applicable.
  • Replacement materials used where applicable.
  • Completion summary, recommendations or referrals where required.

Completion evidence supports the record of work undertaken but does not determine a third party’s lending or valuation decision.
