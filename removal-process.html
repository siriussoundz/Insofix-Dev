Assessment and Remediation Process

The process separates an initial remote review from any chargeable on-site assessment or agreed removal work.

  1. Free remote photo review: send safe photographs, the reason for concern and any survey wording.
  2. Initial discussion: Insofix considers the property, apparent foam type and customer objective.
  3. Chargeable on-site assessment where required: used where photographs cannot answer access, construction, extent or visible-condition questions.
  4. Accessible inspection: roof areas and visible timber condition are reviewed within safe access limits.
  5. Foam identification: open-cell or closed-cell material is identified where reasonably possible.
  6. Options discussion: evidence, monitoring, specialist input, remediation, removal or no immediate removal may be discussed.
  7. Agreed work: removal or remediation proceeds only where appropriate and scoped in writing.
  8. Post-work evidence: relevant photographs, work notes and documentation are provided where applicable.

Insofix is not acting as a mortgage lender, valuer, structural engineer or legal adviser. Documentation can support a case, but it cannot guarantee third-party approval.
