Privacy Notice

This notice explains how enquiry information may be handled once online submission is enabled.

Who is responsible

Insofix Ltd is responsible for information submitted through its website once submission is enabled. The current online form does not transmit information.

Information likely to be collected

Enquiry information may include your name, contact details, property location, reason for enquiry, photographs, survey extracts and notes about potential work.

How information may be used

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, review potential work, arrange assessments, prepare quotations and keep appropriate business records.

Sharing information

Information is not sold. It may be shared with service providers or professional advisers only where necessary for the enquiry, business administration or legal obligations.

Send only relevant details

Please avoid submitting unnecessary financial, identity or unrelated personal information. Survey extracts should be limited to the relevant parts where possible.

Your requests

You may request access, correction or deletion of your information, subject to legal obligations and business-record requirements. Privacy contact details should be added only once verified by Insofix Ltd.
