Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about spray foam removal, mortgage issues, remote review, assessment and documentation.

Is the free remote photo review a survey?

No. It is an initial review of photographs and information, not a survey, valuation, structural assessment or lender decision.

Can documentation guarantee acceptance?

No. Lender, surveyor, valuer and equity-release policies vary and can change. Documentation can support the record of work but cannot guarantee third-party acceptance.

Does Insofix act as a structural engineer?

No structural engineering service is claimed here. Where roofing, timber or structural input is required, the need for appropriate specialist input should be explained.

Do assessment findings have limits?

Yes. Findings depend on safe access, visible conditions, the information supplied and the agreed scope.

Are named accreditations claimed?

No government, lender, RICS, PCA, TrustMark or PAS approval claim is made unless verified evidence is added later.
