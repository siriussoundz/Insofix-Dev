SuperFOIL and Replacement Insulation

Replacement insulation is considered after the removal area, construction and ventilation needs are understood.

What multi-layer reflective insulation is

Multi-layer reflective insulation uses layers of foil and insulation material as part of a designed build-up. SuperFOIL may be considered for some roof or loft situations, but the selected system and installation design determine performance.

When it may be considered

It may be discussed where a property needs a replacement approach after removal, where access or depth constraints matter, or where a particular roof build-up suits a reflective layer. Suitability depends on ventilation, fixing method and surrounding construction.

Separate scopes

Removal and replacement are separate decisions. The removal quotation deals with taking material away and preparing the area; a replacement-insulation quote considers what should be installed afterwards and what approvals or specifications are relevant.

Alternative approaches

Some properties may suit mineral wool or other conventional loft insulation instead. The right option depends on how the space is used, existing ventilation, roof condition and whether future inspection access is needed.

Quotation and approval

Any replacement proposal should be set out in writing, including the intended area and product approach. If a buyer, surveyor or lender has requirements, obtain them before choosing a system.
