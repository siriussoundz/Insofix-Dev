Superfoil Insulation

A high-performance, space-saving insulation solution ideal for lofts and properties following spray foam or cavity wall insulation removal.

What is Superfoil?

Superfoil is a multi-layer reflective insulation designed to deliver excellent thermal performance while remaining thin and lightweight.

It is particularly well suited to lofts where traditional insulation may be difficult to install or where airflow and ventilation need to be maintained.

Why Choose Superfoil?

High Thermal Performance

Reflective foil layers help reduce heat loss and improve energy efficiency, keeping homes warmer in winter and cooler in summer.

3-in-1 Protection

Acts as insulation, a vapour control layer and a radiant barrier — helping to reduce condensation, damp and mould risks.

Thin & Space Saving

Multi-layer design provides strong performance without taking up valuable loft or internal space.

Flexible & Easy to Install

Lightweight and adaptable, making it ideal for lofts, retrofits and properties with restricted access.

Designed for Modern Standards

When installed correctly, systems can achieve high performance levels, supporting improved energy efficiency across the home.

Suitable for Multiple Areas

Can be used in roofs, walls and floors, making it a versatile solution following insulation removal.

Advanced Insulation Technology

Superfoil insulation uses multiple layers of reflective material combined with insulating cores to minimise heat transfer through radiation, convection and conduction.

This layered design allows it to deliver strong thermal performance while remaining lightweight and practical for installation in existing properties.

When is Superfoil Used?

After Spray Foam Removal

Provides a clean, modern insulation solution once foam has been removed.

After Cavity Wall Issues

Used as part of broader insulation upgrades in affected properties.

Loft Upgrades

Improves thermal performance while maintaining airflow and access.

Property Improvements

Suitable for homeowners looking to modernise insulation systems.
