Chargeable On-Site Assessment

When a chargeable on-site assessment may be needed after the free remote photo review.

Purpose

An on-site assessment is used where photographs cannot answer questions about access, foam type, roof construction, visible condition, extent or the customer objective.

What may be reviewed

Accessible roof areas, visible timber condition, ventilation context, foam extent and practical removal or remediation risks may be considered within safe access limits.

Commercial terms

Any chargeable assessment fee or travel arrangement should be confirmed before booking. No price, VAT treatment or fee-credit arrangement is published here until confirmed.

Limits

Assessment findings depend on access and visible conditions. Requirements vary, so customers should confirm the evidence needed with the relevant lender, adviser or surveyor.
