Service Areas

Insofix considers enquiries across England, with initial priority availability focused across Southern England.

England-wide enquiries

Homeowners across England can start with a remote review. This is especially useful where a property is outside the priority area, because photographs and documents help decide whether a visit is sensible before travel is planned.

Southern England priority

Initial priority availability is focused across Southern England, including Hampshire, Surrey, Sussex, Berkshire, Dorset, Wiltshire, Greater London and Oxfordshire. These are service examples, not branch-office locations.

Longer-distance work

For more distant properties, travel time, access arrangements or mobilisation may need to be included in the quotation. Any additional charge should be discussed before a visit is booked.

Checking availability in your area

Send the postcode, property type, reason for enquiry and a few safe photographs. Insofix can then confirm whether the enquiry can be reviewed remotely, whether an on-site assessment is realistic and what travel considerations apply.
