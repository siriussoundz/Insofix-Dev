Free Initial Photo Review

A remote review helps decide whether an on-site assessment or quotation route is realistic.

What customers can send

Useful items include safe wide-angle photographs of the loft or wall area, close-ups of visible foam or insulation, the loft access, roof timbers, labels, invoices and relevant survey extracts. Do not enter unsafe spaces to take them.

What Insofix reviews

The review looks at the apparent material, rough extent, visible access, construction clues and the reason for your enquiry. It is an initial sorting step, not a full diagnosis.

What the customer receives

The response may ask for more photographs, suggest a phone conversation, recommend a chargeable on-site assessment, advise independent specialist input, or explain a likely route towards quotation.

What it is not

The free review is not a structural survey, formal diagnosis, mortgage valuation, damp report or lender decision. It cannot reveal every hidden issue or guarantee that another organisation will accept a particular outcome.

Photograph safety

Do not climb unsupported ladders, step outside safe boarded areas, disturb electrics, pull at materials, damage insulation or expose yourself to dust. If safe photographs are not possible, say so.

Privacy

Photos and documents should show only relevant property information. Remove unnecessary personal, identity or financial details before sharing any paperwork.

Next steps

After the review, Insofix may be able to discuss a quotation route or may recommend an on-site assessment first. The assessment fee, if needed, is confirmed before booking.
